Why we’re different

We’re all flavor, no filler. Pizza we feel good about serving. Pizza you feel good about eating. Pizza that puts itself out there in all of its naked glory.

fresh

we freshly prepare our ingredients

We do our best to ensure that our cheese and vegetables are carefully sourced. Our meats are pork, chicken and beef have no bad stuff hiding in them (that we know of)— but are full of love. We freshly slice ingredients for our pizzas in-restaurant — from tomatos, to jalapeños and apples.

Ancestral blend crust

Our crust is made from an Ancestral Blend® of 10 grains

Our crust is made from an Ancestral Blend® of 10 grains plus prebiotic agave fiber and probiotics (healthful bacteria like the ones found in yogurt for balance and digestive health) bound by water and made by hand. Eating NKD pizza can leave you feeling really satisfied. We believe it’s the diversity of grains, fiber and probiotics that can help provide a slow, sustained release of energy avoiding the crash some people experience after eating single grain, highly processed pizzas.

NKD tomato sauce

our very own house-made tomato sauce

Our tomato sauce is deliciously spiced and herbed, with no added sugar or citric acid. Nothing processed or modified or tinkered with (not that we know of). No additives or preservatives. No artificial colors or flavors. No, none of that. Just real fresh ingredients we combine to make really tasty pizza.

Ancestral blend crust

Our crust is made from an Ancestral Blend® of 10 grains

Our crust is made from an Ancestral Blend® of 10 grains plus prebiotic agave fiber and probiotics (healthful bacteria like the ones found in yogurt for balance and digestive health) bound by water and made by hand. A diversity of grains, fiber and probiotics are the main reason for that satisfying feeling after eating NKD Pizza, contributing a slow, sustained release of energy without the crash from eating other single grain, highly processed pizzas.
